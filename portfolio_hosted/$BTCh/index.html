What is $BTCh?

Introducing Bitcoin Halving 2024 ($BTCH), the innovative token on the BNB Smart Chain that celebrates the pivotal Bitcoin halving event. $BTCH is designed to honor this momentous occasion by rewarding holders with BTCb dividends (the BEP-20 token pegged to the price of Bitcoin), based on the duration and quantity of their holdings. This unique mechanism incentivizes long-term investment and participation within the cryptocurrency community, directly linking the excitement of Bitcoin's reduced mining reward event to tangible benefits for its supporters. With BitcoinHalving, you can celebrate the halving event in style, joining a forward-thinking community on the BNB Smart Chain that appreciates the significance of scarcity in the digital currency landscape.


What is Bitcoin Halving?

Bitcoin halving is a critical event in the Bitcoin protocol that halves the reward for mining new blocks approximately every four years, or every 210,000 blocks. This reduction in the new supply rate often leads to bullish sentiment in the market, as past halvings have typically preceded significant increases in Bitcoin's price. The mechanism aims to control inflation and ensure the total supply never exceeds 21 million, making Bitcoin more scarce and potentially more valuable over time, similar to precious metals like gold.
